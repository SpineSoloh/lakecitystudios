About Us

Welcome to Lake City Music School, a leading center for music education and training based in Kisumu. Founded in 2022, our institute is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of musicians, performers, composers, and educators.

Our programs cater to students of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to advanced musicians, offering courses in a wide range of instruments, music theory, composition, production, and performance.

Our Mission

At Lake City Music School, our mission is to inspire and educate students by fostering creativity, discipline, and excellence in the field of music. We strive to provide a nurturing environment that supports students in achieving their musical and personal goals.

Programs and Courses

Instrumental Training

Professional instruction in piano, guitar, violin, drums, wind instruments, and more, led by expert musicians.

Voice Lessons

Vocal training that focuses on technique, tone, and stage presence, with specialized coaching for various music genres.

Music Theory & Composition

Courses covering the foundations of music theory, harmony, ear training, and songwriting.

Music Production & Technology

Learn the art of music production, sound engineering, and digital music creation using industry-standard software.

Performance Arts

Ensemble classes, bands, and choirs that provide students with live performance opportunities and stage experience.

Jazz, Classical, and Contemporary Programs

Specialized programs focusing on specific music genres and styles.

Our Facilities

Lake City Music School offers top-notch facilities to enhance the learning experience, including a fully equipped recording studio, soundproofed practice rooms, an instrument library, and a technology lab.
